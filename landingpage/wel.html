What is WebHunt?

Hackathons are events put together to bring the coding community together. They’re typically over a weekend. “Hackers” get together and pitch ideas. People then split up into teams and work on turning the ideas into apps.

Software Freedom Day

Software Freedom Day (SFD) is an annual worldwide celebration of Free Software. SFD is a public education effort with the aim of increasing awareness of Free Software and its virtues, and encouraging its use.
